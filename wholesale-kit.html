Why Retailers Carry BSFL

  • Broad species appeal: suitable as a supplemental treat for poultry, ornamental birds, wild birds, fish, and reptiles.
  • Nutrient-dense ingredient: whole dried BSFL naturally contain protein, fat, calcium, phosphorus, and beneficial insect-based structural compounds such as chitin.
  • Sustainability story: BSFL are part of a circular nutrition model that can transform organic byproducts into valuable animal nutrition.
  • Easy retail education: simple feeding directions and strong shelf presence make the product easy for customers to understand and try.
