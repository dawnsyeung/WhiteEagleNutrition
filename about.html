Our origin story

We exist to create a virtuous cycle that extends animal health spans and preserves our natural resources.

White Eagle Nutrition was founded by integrative nutritionists and insect agriculture experts who wanted better answers than conventional feed. We craft USA-sourced BSFL-based foods that are ethical, science-backed, and designed to keep rescued nutrients, thriving animals, and restored ecosystems reinforcing one another‚Äîfrom arthritic horses to aquatic turtles.

Our mission

Our mission is to create and continuously fulfill a virtuous cycle where upcycled nutrients become precision BSFL nutrition, animals thrive longer, and the resulting wellbeing fortifies soils, research, and community care.

  • Co-create with regenerative farms to upcycle food waste into premium BSFL feed and return finished compost to their soils.
  • Transform those inputs into species-specific routines guided by veterinary science and continuous data analysis.
  • Equip guardians with consults and education that convert healthier animals into lighter vet bills and calmer households.
  • Reinvest time, revenue, and learnings into research grants, welfare partnerships, and infrastructure that accelerates the cycle.
