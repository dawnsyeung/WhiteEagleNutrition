Our origin story

We exist to extend animal health spans and protect our planet.

White Eagle Nutrition was founded by integrative nutritionists and insect agriculture experts who wanted better answers than conventional feed. We craft BSFL-based foods that are ethical, science-backed, and designed to reduce medical bills by nurturing resilient gut and immune systems.

Our mission

Deliver functional BSFL nutrition that extends the healthy years of pets while regenerating the ecosystems we all depend on. We believe in precision formulations, radical transparency, and compassionate stewardship for every animal in a multi-species household.

  • Formulate with clinical science and veterinarian input.
  • Source from vertically integrated, waste-upcycling BSFL farms.
  • Invest in rigorous quality assurance for safety and efficacy.
  • Educate guardians on nutrition that prevents disease, not just treats it.

Guiding values

Animal-first formulating

We analyze species biology to deliver precise amino and fatty acid profiles for longevity.

Evidence obsessed

Our R&D team partners with universities to publish digestibility and immune studies on BSFL proteins.

Regenerative supply

Larvae are raised on upcycled produce in zero-waste facilities powered by renewable energy.

Accessible wellness

Auto-ship bundles, community grants, and shelter partnerships keep animal wellness within reach.
