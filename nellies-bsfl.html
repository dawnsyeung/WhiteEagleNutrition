White Eagle Nutrition bulk supply division

Nellie's BSFL supports bulk feed and private label programs from sample stage to recurring volume.

Nellie's BSFL is part of White Eagle Nutrition and focuses on procurement-ready BSFL supply for farms, feed teams, resellers, and brands. The program supports whole larvae, protein ingredients, and frass products with pathways for retail, pallet-scale, recurring wholesale, and private label launch support.

Built for buyers who need dependable feed supply

The Nellie's BSFL model is designed for practical procurement. Buyers can start with retail or samples, then transition to pallet, bulk, or recurring programs as volumes increase.

Core product families include whole BSFL larvae for poultry and specialty feeding routines, BSFL protein ingredients for feed and nutrition programs, and frass for soil and turf systems.

The ordering flow supports format selection, monthly volume planning, logistics alignment, and private label onboarding for qualified partners that want to sell under their own brand.

Product pathways for bulk feed and private label

Whole BSFL

Whole dried larvae for poultry, reptiles, wild birds, and specialty feed programs in retail and bulk formats.

BSFL protein

Protein-rich ingredient options for aquaculture, pet nutrition, poultry programs, and feed-trial initiatives.

Frass fertilizer

Microbial-rich frass for greenhouse, turf, crop, and garden use with both retail and wholesale availability.

Sample-to-scale workflow

Validate with sample shipments first, then move into recurring volume supply once specs and fit are confirmed.

Documentation support

Lot-level QA and procurement documentation can be provided to support sourcing and receiving workflows.

Private label onboarding

Qualified resellers can request packaging, branding, and launch-timeline support for private label pathways.
